Memberships

How to play at Karori Badminton Club

OPTION 1: Purchase a Stadium Pass

The brand new Stadium Pass will work like a gym membership, with an active pass, players can book courts to play as often as they like, with some restrictions during peak hours to allow for fair and equitable access. Players are allowed to book courts to play singles (2-3 players) or doubles (4+ players), and all players for the booking must have an active pass, otherwise a casual court hire fee will be charged and paid in advance of the booking. All Stadium Pass holders (subject to the Stadium Pass Terms & Conditions) are also member of Karori Badminton in accordance with the Karori Badminton Associations Rules. Therefore, you will be able to enter all Badminton New Zealand sanctioned events, special discounts on shuttle purchases, and discounted entry to Karori Badminton hosted competitions and events.

OPTION 2: Join a Club

There is a club to suit every age, ability and background including those that have never played before. Clubs will accept both casual players and those that wish to become a member. For school-aged kids there are several junior programmes to choose from, including our popular Shuttle Time Programmes and a Junior Club. As a club member, you are also a member of Karori Badminton in accordance with the Karori Badminton Association Rules. Therefore, you will be able to enter all Badminton New Zealand sanctioned events, special discounts on shuttle purchases, and discounted entry to Karori Badminton hosted competitions and events.

OPTION 3: Casual Play

Casual players and walk-ins are welcome during our office hours. If you are planning to play outside our office hours, all players must register a FREE account which will allow for 24/7 access and the ability to pre-book online. Casual rate includes free use of racquets and shuttles so no need to spend any money on buying equipment (during office hours only).
